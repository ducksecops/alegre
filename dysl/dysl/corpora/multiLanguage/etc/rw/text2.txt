Indimi Wikipediya ibonekamo

Iyi Wikipediya yanditswe mu Kinyarwanda. Kuva yatangira mu 2001, ifite kugeza ubu inyandiko zigera kuri 1,787 articles. Hariho izindi Wikipediya nyinshi; zimwe muri zo zigaragaraho kuba nini cyane zagaragajwe hasi aha.



Nyuma y’aho mu minsi ishize abakoresha Facebook batangiye kubona ko ingano y’inyuguti umuntu yanditse cyangwa izo abandi banditse zagabanutse, bamwe bakagira ngo ni ikibazo cy’imashini bakoresha cyangwa browser (Mozilla, IE, 360, Google Chrome,…) bakoreramo.

Nyuma y’aho ibibajijwe kenshi biciye kuri Twitter rero, Facebook yasohotse itangazo nayo ibicishije kuri Twitter, aho yavuze ko uko kugabanya ingano y’inyuguti (font size) biri mu rwego rwo gukomeza guhindura uburyo iyo site iteye n’ibyo igeza ku bakunzi bayo kugirango serivisi zayo zikomeze kubanogera.
